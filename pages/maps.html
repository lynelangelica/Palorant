Obsidian

A top-secret research facility shattered by a failed radianite experiment. With defensive options as divided as the map itself, the choice is yours: confront the attackers on their ground or fortify your position to withstand the assault.

Nebula

Attackers advance into the Defenders on this two-site map, set in a lively underwater city. Nebula is a geo-focused map with no special mechanics. Engage in combat through a tight mid or the long-range flanks in our first map set in Omega Earth.

Frostbite

Your next battlefield is a hidden Kingdom excavation site, now claimed by the arctic. The two plant sites, shielded by snow and steel, demand precise horizontal movement. Utilize the ziplines to catch your enemies off guard.

Pinnacle

Pinnacle is an open battleground for tactical skirmishes, with two sites split by key positioning. One-way bomb doors lock sites and must be destroyed or bypassed. Hold your ground, yield little.

Crimson

Deep beneath a long-forgotten monastery, chaos erupts as rival Agents battle for control over three sites. With more ground to cover, defenders can turn the extra space to their advantage with bold, aggressive pushes.
